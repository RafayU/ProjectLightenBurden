Project Lighten Burden

We are a student-run organization that provides support to our beloved community by encouraging and providing students with volunteer opportunities run by our team to better our neighborhoods. We aim to build lasting leadership qualities in like-minded students and changing narratives about current students by helping everyone. By building a stronger community and provide service to ourselves and others we will be rewarded for our proud actions.
